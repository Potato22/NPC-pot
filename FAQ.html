What is NusaPonyCon?

NusaPonyCon is Indonesia’s first ever convention dedicated to My Little Pony: Friendship is Magic. It is an event that is aimed at fans of the show from every demography. Regardless of your age, gender, or where you come from, we welcome you to join the archipelago’s celebration of friendship.

When is NusaPonyCon going to happen?

NusaPonyCon will take place on 13th of July, 2019 in the National Museum of Indonesia, Jakarta.

Extraordinary amounts of research and planning were taken place to pick this date, and we have secured the venue for our event on the 13th of July. What is My Little Pony?

My Little Pony is a brand of toys that is owned by Hasbro. Since its inception in the 1980s, the My Little Pony brand has been advocating a line of toys that revolve around the world of magical talking ponies. My Little Pony itself has been through several generations of the franchise. My Little Pony: Friendship is Magic, the newest iteration of the brand, was first piloted in October 2010 following the vision and design of Lauren Faust, who has been known for her past works in other animated shows such as Powerpuff Girls and Foster’s Home for Imaginary Friends. Faust’s vision allows a new art and storytelling style that is contemporary to the classic My Little Pony franchise, which aligns it towards newer designs of cartoon characters aimed at refactoring the brand and providing fresh, a new take on the franchise. My Little Pony: Friendship is Magic is currently the most successful iteration of the brand, and have continued to nine seasons, with a theater movie and a related spinoff, Equestria Girls. Friendship is Magic forms the primary locus of NusaPonyCon, as the community of fans of the show is the one that mobilized such a massive interest group worldwide, including in Indonesia. We do, however, welcome every fan and content from across all the My Little Pony franchise!

What are bronies?

Bronies, a portmanteau of “bro” and “pony”, is a term that is related to fans of My Little Pony: Friendship is Magic in general. Bronies Nowadays, the brony fandom has morphed into a diverse society, by demographics and by geographics, built and sustained by social media and internet-based mediums of interaction across the globe. With bronies sustaining friendships between land and sea, and beyond borders and miles, it is a community that proudly bolsters the values of friendships without prejudice.

Why run a convention?

The idea of conventions was brought to life in 2011. Originally intended as a platform to congregate communities and show staff, it also became a trademark platform where bronies can foster social connections, grow their networks, exhibit or even sell their arts and crafts, and even as a stage to perform and tell the world of their projects, skills, and talents. Now, in nations worldwide, conventions became the greatest supporting pillars of the fandom, as a platform to share, communicate, exhibit, and perform to the community. But most importantly, it became a place for people to congregate and share from one interest, and foster networks and friendships to last. NusaPonyCon believes that Indonesia does not need to miss out from this experience, and we intend to bring it home in the best way possible.

In the unlikely event that the event is canceled, any funds that you have paid to our official ticketing site will be refunded to you within fourteen working days of our announcement. What do I get after I purchase my ticket?

You will receive a confirmation email and an electronic ticket generated by Loket.com that contains your proof of purchase and ticket. To redeem your ticket, you will have to bring this electronic ticket to the event. You will then be checked in and your ticket will be swapped with an official convention badge that confirms your attendance on the day. You can either show the ticket on your device or print it out for our check-in crew to inspect upon entry. When your ticket is redeemed, your badge gives multiple entries towards the venue - no more queueing or re-registering required when you leave and reenter the venue, as many times as you like!
